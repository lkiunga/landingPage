Skating is awesome

Just like life skating teaches you to be happy, carefree and with a calm heart. coz if you gonna roll the blade you might as well come the fucl down, I know it sounds crazy but there is no way you gonna roll down the hill with or do a 180 /360 with a clumsy heart. sometimes you call yourself to a meeting and go like breath 1, breathe2 and breathe 3 then relax and come on you can do it. Types of skating

Roller skating - glide across a hard surface on for wheels

Inline skating - Glide across a hard surface but on 3 wheels

Ice skating glide across ice

Skate boarding - Glide across a hard surface on skate board
